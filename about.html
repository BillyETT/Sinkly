Our Story

Started in Toronto. Still here.

Sinkly was founded by Billy and Ali — two business partners who spent years in the home renovation industry and kept running into the same problem: great-looking fixtures that didn't hold up, and quality products that were impossible to find in one place.

So they built it themselves. Starting out of Toronto nearly ten years ago, they set out to offer sinks and faucets that were worth the investment — products chosen for how they perform, not just how they photograph.

Today, Sinkly carries everything from stainless steel kitchen sinks and vessel basins to fully custom stone sinks, all delivered Canada-wide. The business has grown, but the approach hasn't changed: stock products that actually last, and stand behind them.
